Our Vision and Values

At Trace, our vision is to create a seamless digital ecosystem where media is easily traceable and protected, empowering creators and businesses to focus on their craft. What We Do

Trace's software is designed for precision, scalability, and speed. Our AI-powered system quickly identifies visual patterns in video content, matching them against extensive databases for real-time detection. This enables media companies to manage assets, identify unauthorized use, and offer smarter content recommendations.
